Our Mission

As debators, our mission is to become better as debators while teaching other and ourselves about the world policy, advocacy, and current events.

day in the life of a debator

In the case of Theo: "I wake up, write blocks, do speaking drills debate, learn about the world, eat, write more blocks, eat, do rebuttal redos, do speaking drills, do rebuttal redos again, then sleep, and repeat"

In the case of Rachel: "As a (SAMWMS) debater, you wake up ridiculously early on the weekends. You take a train to some school. You do speaking drills and hang out with your teammates, some of whom are your best friends. You prep arguments about the world around you. You debate against people older than you, younger than you, the same age as you. You learn how to use personal attacks against you to your advantage. You argue about what debate and the world should be like, and when you finish your round, you try and make debate and the world like that."
