Speaking Drills



Clarity


Over Enunciation

Over enunciation is a drill where you speak out everyword as clearly as possible, emphasizing every syllable.


Word Drills

Word Drills are where you read the evidence with a word in between each word like "a" or "and".


Accents

Accents are where you read the evidence with an accent.


Backwards

Backwards is a drill where you read the evidence backwards.


Pen Drills

Pen Drills are where you read the evidence with a pen in your mouth.



Speed


Straight Down

Straight Down is a drill where you read the evidence as fast as you can as though you were giving a speech.



Familiarization


Read Through

Read through is a drill where you read the evidence at a normal speed and tone.



More


Breathing Drill

Breathing drill is where you read as much of the evidence with one breath. Try to breath from your driaphraghm rather than your lungs to you can get more words in with one breath.


Emotion Drill

Read through is a drill where you read the evidence at a normal speed and tone.
